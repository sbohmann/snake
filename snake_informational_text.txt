Due to the nagging of prior users, I have relented and made the decision to provide this informational text.

At the beginng, the snake is the black dot in the top left corner.

The left arrow key makes the snake move left.
The up arrow key makes the snake move up.
The right arrow key makes the snake move right.
The down arrow key makes the snake move down.
The left arrow key makes the snake move left.
The space arrow key stops the snake.
The escape arrow key restarts the game.

The red dot is the apple. Eat the apple to gain scores.

Do not leave the confines of the rectangle.

When the rectangle turns red, you have lost and need to restart.

The escape arrow key restarts the game.
